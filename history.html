Discover the Rich History of Seberida!

Welcome to the captivating history of Seberida, a village nestled in the heart of the Batang Gansal sub-district within the enchanting Indragiri Hulu Regency. Let's embark on a journey through time to explore the vibrant tapestry that makes Seberida a truly unique and fascinating community.

Inauguration and Diverse Roots

The story of Seberida begins around 1918 AD when Muhammad ISA, the first Wali Negeri, assumed leadership. Since its inception, Seberida has been a melting pot of diverse ethnicities, including Malays, Javanese, Sundanese, Batak, and various other migrant communities. This diversity has become a strength, portraying unity in diversity and fostering a harmonious balance among the coexisting communities.

Leadership Transitions and Regional Transformations

Over the years, the village has witnessed ten changes in leadership. These leaders not only represented their respective eras but also played a pivotal role in the transformation of the region. Seberida evolved from a vast area encompassing Seberida sub-district to administrative entities such as Seberida village and Kota Baru Seberida.

The Origin of "Seberida"

The name "Seberida" finds its origin in the intriguing tale of the abundant "belida" fish in the Seberida River. Over time, the name underwent linguistic transformations, becoming the distinctive identity of the village—symbolizing abundance and sustainable living.

Leadership Dedication and Community Building

From Muhammad ISA to the current village head, Ria Saprina, each leader dedicated themselves to making Seberida a better place for its residents. The village looks towards a future full of hope, built upon its rich history and diverse community.

Towards a Promising Future

With a rich history and a diverse community, Seberida strides confidently into a future filled with promise. The historical legacy serves as a robust foundation, guiding the community in building a bright future for generations to come.

Seberida Village, a living story that continues to evolve, dedicates itself to remaining an integral part of heritage and sustainability. Enjoy the journey through the long and fascinating history of Seberida!
